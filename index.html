About

A neighborhood library for modern students

Rooted in Chowhatta, focused on making school life easier for families across Sylhet.

Our story

Shahadat Library is a local book and stationery shop based in Ananda Banijjo Bhaban, opposite Manru Shopping City, Chowhatta, Sylhet. We focus on one simple promise: the right books, at the right time, without confusion.

By keeping up-to-date book lists for schools like Scholarsome and Anandaniketan School, we help parents avoid last-minute rush and missing items.

Why parents choose us

  • Updated school book lists – aligned with current syllabi.
  • Complete sets – textbooks, guides and matching stationery.
  • Fair pricing – clear, itemized billing.
  • Friendly guidance – help for new parents and students.
